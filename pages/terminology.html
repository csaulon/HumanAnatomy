Anatomical Terms

Median Plane

divides the body into equal right and left

Sagittal Plane

a plane that is parrallel to the median plane

Transverse Plane

divides the body into top and bottom

Frontal Plane

divides the body into front and back

Anterior/ Ventral

in front of

Posterior/ Dorsal

in back of

Cranial/ Superior

toward the head or above

Caudal/Inferior

toward the feet or below

Medial

toward the midline

Lateral

away from the midline

Proximal

closets to the attachment of the trunk or point of origin

Distal

furthest from the attachment of the trunk or point of origin

Superficial

on the surface

Deep

below the surface
